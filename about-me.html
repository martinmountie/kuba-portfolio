Jakub (Kuba) Trzciński

Born: 1996 in Mrągowo,
Warmian-Masurian, Poland
Currently working in Poznan.

Studies: 2015 Collegium Da Vinci
Poznan, 2017 University of Arts Poznan

Awards: 1th place ona nationwide painting plein-air in Kazimierz Dolny 2018, qualification for SHORT-LIST at the TYPOMANIA festival in Moscow, 2018

Exhibitions: 2017, exhibition of drawing, Galeria OKNO, ul. Szewska, Poznań 2018, Strength of the String, Galeria NAD SCHODAMI, University of Arts in Poznan

Few words: I am currently working in Poznan. I deal mostly with painting and graphic, but also animations, publications and objects. In 2018 most of my work has been divided into „The Art of Bicycle”, „Anti-junk Work” and painting.

If I had to mention three names that I am interested in, it would be: Rothko, Basquiat, Soulages, Kline, Klein, Rauchenberg, Bereś
